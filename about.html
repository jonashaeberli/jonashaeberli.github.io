About Me

Hi, I'm Jonas Häberli — a passionate mechanical engineer with a strong focus on robotics and automation.

My professional journey began in 2020 with an apprenticeship as a design engineer. In Switzerland, this hands-on education path provides a nationally recognized qualification, offering direct entry into the engineering field. During my apprenticeship, I gained early and practical exposure to mechanical design and development. In 2024, I graduated as the top-performing design engineering apprentice in the canton of Bern.

Alongside my apprenticeship, I earned two additional academic certifications — the Berufsmaturität and the Passerelle — which allow me to study at a Swiss university without attending traditional secondary school.

After completing the apprenticeship, I continued working at the same company, contributing to the development of a fully automated packaging machine. My role focused primarily on conceptual and design engineering tasks. Following this project, I completed my mandatory military service in the Swiss Army before transitioning to academic studies.

In 2025, I’ll begin studying Mechanical Engineering at ETH Zurich, where I plan to deepen my expertise and continue developing innovative robotic and automation systems.
